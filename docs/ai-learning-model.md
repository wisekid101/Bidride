# AI & Learning Model

> **Status: PLACEHOLDER — To be developed**

## Purpose
BidRide will use data and machine learning to continuously improve the marketplace for both riders and drivers.

## Potential Applications

### Pricing Intelligence
- Learn optimal bid ranges by route, time of day, and demand
- Predict likelihood a bid will be accepted
- Surface "smart bid" suggestions to riders

### Driver Matching
- Prioritize drivers most likely to accept based on historical behavior
- Reduce wait time through smarter dispatch

### Demand Forecasting
- Predict high-demand zones (EWR flight arrivals, events, etc.)
- Alert drivers to move to high-demand areas

### Safety & Fraud
- Anomaly detection on ride patterns
- Identify suspicious behavior

### Personalization
- Rider: remember preferred bid ranges
- Driver: learn preferred routes, times, ride types

## Data Requirements
- Ride history
- Bid/counter/accept/reject outcomes
- Location and time data
- Driver and rider ratings
