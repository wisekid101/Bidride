# Pricing Model

> **Status: DRAFT — Requires validation and legal review**

## Overview
BidiRide uses a dual-mode pricing model: standard fare (instant) and bid-based negotiation (optional).

## Standard Fare (Mode 1)
- Calculated using base fare + per-mile rate + per-minute rate + demand multiplier
- Familiar to riders coming from Uber/Lyft
- Driver accepts and goes — no negotiation needed

## Bid Mode (Mode 2)
- Rider sees the standard fare and can submit a lower bid
- Bid has a floor (minimum acceptable amount — protects driver earnings)
- Driver can:
  - **Accept** the bid
  - **Reject** the bid (rider falls back to standard fare or cancels)
  - **Counter** with a different price
- Negotiation rounds: TBD (1-2 counter rounds max to keep experience fast)

## Platform Revenue Model
- Commission per completed ride (% of fare)
- Rate TBD — target competitive with or below Uber/Lyft driver commission split
- Potential: surge/peak pricing during high-demand periods

## Driver Earnings Philosophy
- Drivers keep a higher percentage of fares than current industry standard
- Bid floor protects drivers from below-minimum rides
- Counter-offer gives drivers agency
