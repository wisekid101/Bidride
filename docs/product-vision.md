# Product Vision

## The Problem
Current rideshare platforms fail both sides of the marketplace:
- Riders pay high, unpredictable prices
- Drivers receive a smaller percentage of fares than they deserve
- Neither side has meaningful control or transparency

## The Solution: BidiRide
A rideshare marketplace that combines the instant-request UX of Uber/Lyft with an optional bidding layer — giving both riders and drivers more control.

## How It Works
1. Rider opens app and requests a ride (same experience as Uber/Lyft)
2. Rider can **accept the standard fare** (instant match) OR **submit a lower bid**
3. If a bid is submitted, drivers can **accept, reject, or counter**
4. Both sides negotiate to a fair price — or fall back to standard fare

**Bidding is optional. The standard experience is always available.**

## Core Value Propositions
| Riders | Drivers |
|--------|---------|
| Save money through bidding | Earn more — better fare share |
| Price transparency | Control over which rides to accept |
| Familiar UX | Counter-offer capability |

## Launch Market
- **Phase 1:** Newark, New Jersey + Newark Airport (EWR)
- Dominate one market before expanding
- Learn, improve, then scale

## Core Principles (Priority Order)
1. Safety First
2. Legality First
3. Trust First
4. Driver Success
5. Rider Savings
6. Transparency
7. Sustainability
